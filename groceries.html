Grocery Shopping

The options below are the most common grocery stores and delivery services among University of Michigan student.

Grocery Stores

Here are grocery stores that are popular amongst students.

Meijer ($$)

Overview

If you are looking for anything organic and willing to pay the price, Whole Foods is where you should be heading to.

Pros

+ Organic
+ Public Transportation
+ Large Selection

Cons

+ Distance
+ Expensive

Find nearby store on Google Maps.

Target ($$)

Overview

Located at the heart of the Central Campus, State st. Target is the go-to place for a lot of students when they need to buy something small.

Pros

+ Proximity
+ Decent-sized Stores

Cons

+ Tad bit more expensive
+ Busy during moving season

Find nearby store on Google Maps.

Walmart ($$)

Overview

Known for selling bulk grocery items as well as other products. It is convenient for big families or people who live in a big house.

Pros

+ Saving Money
+ Public Transportation
+ Large Selection

Cons

+ Distance

Find nearby store on Google Maps.

Costco ($$)

Overview

Known for selling bulk grocery items as well as other products. It is convenient for big families or people who live in a big house.

Pros

+ Saving Money
+ Cheap Clothing
+ Large Selection

Cons

+ No public transportation
+ Annual Fee ($60)
+ Distance

Find nearby store on Google Maps.
